## Welcome to the SDX Newsletter

This is the inaugural issue of the SDX monthly newsletter. Each month, we share updates on the platform, governance, partnerships, and the product roadmap — published on the last Thursday of every month.

## Platform Launch

SDX is now live. The platform is completely free for all users — owners, consultants, and third-party software providers can read from and write to SDX at zero cost. We launched with support for 60+ property types across 30+ countries, with six global benchmarking standards computed simultaneously from a single data submission.

## Governance Charter Ratified

The SDX Board has ratified the founding charter. Key commitments include voluntary and free participation for data contributors, unconditional data portability and exit rights, and a commercialization boundary that prohibits the sale of raw, owner-identifiable data. The charter can only be amended by owner supermajority.

## Product Highlights

- **Multi-standard benchmarking**: SDX, ENERGY STAR, NABERS, EU EPC, CRREM, and GRESB scores from one submission
- **Data quality scoring**: Completeness, coverage, and freshness grades with letter ratings
- **Automated utility feeds**: Connections to 35+ global utility providers plus Green Button compatibility

## What's Next

In February, we will be expanding API capabilities for third-party integrators and onboarding the first wave of authorized representatives. The Product Committee is finalizing normalization methodology for weather-adjusted source EUI across all supported climate zones.
