# Welcome to SDX

SDX (Sustainability Data Exchange) is a not-for-profit, industry-governed platform that provides free tools for real estate sustainability data management, benchmarking, and reporting.

## Who is SDX for?

SDX serves three audiences:

- **Data Contributors** — Real estate owners, operators, and their service providers who submit building performance data
- **Third-Party Integrators** — Software platforms that integrate with SDX to read and write data on behalf of owners
- **Licensed Content Buyers** — Investors, lenders, and index providers who purchase board-approved aggregate data products

## The platform is free

There are no fees to use SDX. Everyone — owners, service providers, and third-party software platforms — can read from and write to the platform at zero cost. The only monetization comes from board-approved licensed content sold to third parties who wish to consume aggregate data products.

Data contributors receive all licensed content free for their own internal use, plus a share of the revenue generated from third-party sales through the [Data Dividend](/docs/data-dividend).

## What you can do with SDX

- **Benchmark** your buildings against six global standards simultaneously (SDX, ENERGY STAR, NABERS, EU EPC, CRREM, GRESB)
- **Report** against 16+ regulatory and voluntary frameworks
- **Audit** your data quality with completeness, coverage, and freshness scoring
- **Integrate** with 35+ utility providers for automated meter data
- **Connect** with existing software platforms via open APIs
