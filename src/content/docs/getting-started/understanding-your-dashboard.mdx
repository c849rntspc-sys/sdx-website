# Understanding Your Dashboard

Once you have added properties and submitted data, the SDX dashboard gives you a single view of your portfolio's sustainability performance.

## Dashboard layout

The dashboard is divided into four sections accessible from the top navigation:

1. **Portfolio Overview** — High-level KPIs across all your buildings
2. **Building Detail** — Deep dive into a single building's performance
3. **Data Quality** — Completeness, coverage, and freshness scores
4. **Compliance** — Status of regulatory and voluntary reporting frameworks

## Portfolio Overview

The portfolio view shows aggregate metrics for every active building in your organisation.

### Key performance indicators

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total floor area** | Sum of gross floor area across all buildings |
| **Energy Use Intensity (EUI)** | Portfolio-weighted average in kWh/m²/year |
| **Carbon Intensity** | Portfolio-weighted average in kgCO₂e/m²/year |
| **Water Use Intensity** | Portfolio-weighted average in litres/m²/year |
| **Data Quality Grade** | Aggregate letter grade from A to F |
| **Data Dividend Estimate** | Projected quarterly dividend based on current data |

You can filter the portfolio view by property type, region, fund, or custom tags.

### Charts and trends

- **EUI trend** — Rolling 12-month energy use intensity with year-on-year comparison
- **Carbon breakdown** — Scope 1 vs. Scope 2 emissions by fuel type
- **Benchmark distribution** — Histogram showing how your buildings rank within SDX peer groups
- **Top movers** — Buildings with the largest improvement or deterioration over the last quarter

## Building Detail

Click any building to open its detail page. Here you will find:

- **Benchmark scores** across all applicable standards (SDX, ENERGY STAR, NABERS, EU EPC, CRREM, GRESB). See [Benchmarking Standards](/docs/benchmarking/benchmarking-standards) for details.
- **Meter data** — Time-series charts for each utility meter, with anomaly flags.
- **Year-on-year comparison** — Side-by-side performance for the current and previous reporting year.
- **Targets** — Custom reduction targets with progress tracking.

## Data Quality panel

The Data Quality panel provides a letter grade (A through F) for each building and the portfolio overall. It evaluates three dimensions:

- **Completeness** — What percentage of expected meter readings have been received?
- **Coverage** — Do submitted meters account for whole-building consumption?
- **Freshness** — How recently was the last reading received?

A higher data quality grade increases your [Data Dividend](/docs/data-dividend) and improves benchmark reliability. See [Data Quality Scoring](/docs/benchmarking/data-quality-scoring) for the full methodology.

## Compliance tracker

The compliance tracker shows the status of each regulatory or voluntary framework you have opted into:

- **Green** — Submission complete and accepted
- **Amber** — Submission in progress or data gaps identified
- **Red** — Deadline approaching with incomplete data

Supported frameworks include GRESB, NYC LL97, Boston BERDO, EU EPBD, UK MEES, and more. See [Compliance Frameworks](/docs/benchmarking/compliance-frameworks) for the full list.

## Customising the dashboard

- **Pin buildings** to the top of the portfolio view for quick access.
- **Create custom views** with saved filter and sort combinations.
- **Set alert thresholds** to receive email or webhook notifications when a metric exceeds a limit.
- **Export any view** to PDF or CSV from the top-right menu.

## Navigation shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| `G` then `P` | Go to Portfolio Overview |
| `G` then `B` | Go to Building Detail (last viewed) |
| `G` then `D` | Go to Data Quality |
| `G` then `C` | Go to Compliance |
| `/` | Open global search |
