# Integrator Pricing

If you build software that integrates with SDX to read or write data on behalf of building owners, you pay nothing.

## Everything is free

| Capability | Cost |
|---|---|
| API key provisioning | Free |
| API calls (all endpoints, read and write) | Free |
| Webhook subscriptions | Free |
| Sandbox environment | Free |
| Production environment | Free |
| Technical documentation | Free |
| Developer support during integration | Free |
| Rate limit allocation | Free (generous defaults) |
| Data submitted on behalf of owners | Free |
| Data read on behalf of owners | Free |

## No partnership tiers

SDX does not operate a partner programme with bronze / silver / gold tiers. Every integrator gets the same capabilities, the same rate limits, and the same support.

## No revenue share from integrators

SDX does not charge integrators a revenue share, transaction fee, or referral fee. If you build a commercial product that uses the SDX API, 100% of your product revenue is yours.

## Why it's free for integrators

SDX's mission is to create the most complete real estate sustainability dataset possible. Third-party software platforms are a critical distribution channel — they help building owners get data into SDX without changing their existing workflows. Charging integrators would reduce participation and ultimately shrink the dataset.

SDX earns revenue solely from [licensed content](/docs/pricing/licensed-content) sold to data consumers (investors, lenders, index providers). Integrators help grow the dataset that makes licensed content valuable. It would be counterproductive to charge them.

## What integrators can offer owners

Integrators may want to highlight the following benefits to the building owners they serve:

- **Free benchmarking** — Owners whose data flows through your integration get free benchmarking against six global standards.
- **Data Dividend eligibility** — Owners earn a share of licensed content revenue through the [Data Dividend](/docs/data-dividend). Note that the dividend goes to the building's underlying owner, not the integrator, and each building earns one dividend regardless of how many platforms submit data.
- **Free licensed content** — Owners can access aggregate data products for free for their own internal use.
- **Compliance reporting** — Owners get automated compliance reports across 16+ frameworks.

## Integrator obligations

While integration is free, integrators must comply with the [SDX API Terms of Service](https://sdx.dev/legal/api-terms), which include:

- **Owner authorisation** — You must have explicit authorisation from the building owner before submitting or reading their data.
- **Data accuracy** — You are responsible for the accuracy of data submitted through your integration.
- **Rate limit compliance** — Respect rate limits and implement proper retry logic. See [Rate Limits](/docs/api/rate-limits).
- **Attribution** — Display the SDX attribution badge where data from SDX is shown in your product (badge assets are provided in the developer portal).
