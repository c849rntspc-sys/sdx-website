# Available Products

SDX offers a range of licensed content products across three categories. All products are updated quarterly and delivered in your preferred format.

## Benchmark subscriptions

### SDX Market Benchmark

Aggregate benchmark distributions for all major property use types, segmented by geography and building size.

| Segment | Coverage |
|---|---|
| **Use types** | Office, retail, residential, industrial, hotel, healthcare, education, data centre |
| **Geographies** | Global, Americas, EMEA, APAC, plus 40+ individual countries |
| **Size bands** | Small (< 5,000 m²), medium (5,000–25,000 m²), large (> 25,000 m²) |

**Metrics included:**
- Energy Use Intensity (EUI) — kWh/m²/year
- Carbon Intensity — kgCO₂e/m²/year
- Water Use Intensity — litres/m²/year
- SDX percentile score distribution

For each segment you receive the minimum, 25th percentile, median, 75th percentile, maximum, and building count.

### Custom peer group benchmark

A tailored benchmark dataset built to match the composition of your portfolio. Specify the use types, geographies, and size bands that matter to you, and SDX produces a bespoke peer group analysis.

**Minimum peer group size:** 20 buildings (to ensure anonymisation).

### ENERGY STAR distribution

Aggregate ENERGY STAR score distributions for eligible US and Canadian buildings. Includes percentile bands by use type and CBSA region.

## Market indices

### SDX Global Sustainability Index

A quarterly time-series index tracking the energy and carbon intensity of the global commercial real estate market.

- **History** — Available from 2020 onward
- **Frequency** — Quarterly data points
- **Breakdown** — Overall, by use type, and by region
- **Methodology** — Area-weighted average of all buildings with data quality grade B or above

### SDX Country Indices

Country-level indices for 40+ markets. Each index tracks EUI, carbon intensity, and water use intensity over time.

### SDX Sector Indices

Sector-level indices for each major property use type. Useful for comparing the sustainability trajectory of office vs. retail vs. residential at a global level.

## Data packs

### Anonymised building-level dataset

Individual building records with all identifying information removed. Each record includes:

- Use type
- Country and climate zone
- Floor area band
- Year built band
- EUI, carbon intensity, water use intensity
- SDX benchmark score
- Data quality grade
- CRREM stranding year band

**Record count:** Updated quarterly. Current dataset contains 100,000+ building records.

### Emission factor dataset

The grid and fuel emission factors used in SDX calculations, sourced from IEA, national registries, and utility-specific data.

- **Coverage** — 190+ countries, sub-national where available (e.g. US eGRID regions, Australian states)
- **Frequency** — Updated annually with the latest published factors
- **Format** — CSV and JSON

### Stranding risk dataset

Aggregate CRREM pathway alignment data showing the percentage of buildings in each segment that are stranded (or projected to strand) under 1.5 °C and 2 °C scenarios.

- **Segments** — By use type, country, and decade
- **Metrics** — Percentage stranded, median years to stranding, average carbon gap
