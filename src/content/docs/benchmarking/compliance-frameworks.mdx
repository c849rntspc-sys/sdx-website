# Compliance Frameworks

SDX maps your building data to 16+ regulatory and voluntary frameworks. Once your data is in the platform, generating a compliance report is a one-click process — no re-entry, no reformatting.

## How compliance reporting works

1. **Opt in** — Select the frameworks that apply to your portfolio under **Compliance > Manage Frameworks**.
2. **SDX checks readiness** — The platform evaluates whether each building has the data required by the framework and flags any gaps.
3. **Generate report** — When data is complete, generate the framework-specific report in the required format.
4. **Submit** — Download the report and submit it to the relevant authority, or use SDX's direct submission integration where available.

## Supported frameworks

### Regulatory — United States

| Framework | Jurisdiction | Deadline | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **NYC LL97** | New York City | Annual (May 1) | Carbon emission caps for buildings over 25,000 sq ft. SDX calculates your building's emissions and compares them to the LL97 limit for your occupancy group. |
| **NYC LL84 / LL133** | New York City | Annual (May 1) | Energy and water benchmarking disclosure. SDX can submit directly to the city's online portal. |
| **Boston BERDO** | Boston, MA | Annual (May 15) | Building Energy Reporting and Disclosure Ordinance for buildings over 20,000 sq ft. |
| **Washington DC BEPS** | Washington, DC | Annual | Building Energy Performance Standards with escalating targets. |
| **California AB 802** | California | Annual (June 1) | Whole-building energy benchmarking for commercial and multifamily buildings. |
| **Colorado Benchmarking** | Colorado | Annual (June 1) | Statewide benchmarking for commercial buildings over 50,000 sq ft. |

### Regulatory — International

| Framework | Jurisdiction | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **EU EPBD** | European Union | Energy Performance of Buildings Directive — minimum energy performance standards and certification. |
| **UK MEES** | United Kingdom | Minimum Energy Efficiency Standards — commercial properties must meet EPC band E or above to be let. |
| **NABERS Mandatory Disclosure** | Australia | Commercial office buildings over 1,000 m² must disclose a NABERS energy rating at point of sale or lease. |
| **Singapore BCA** | Singapore | Building and Construction Authority energy benchmarking for commercial buildings. |

### Voluntary

| Framework | Scope | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **GRESB** | Global | Real estate ESG benchmark for investors. SDX generates the performance component data for your annual GRESB submission. See [GRESB details](/docs/benchmarking/benchmarking-standards). |
| **CDP** | Global | Carbon Disclosure Project — SDX generates the buildings-related sections of your CDP Climate Change questionnaire response. |
| **TCFD / ISSB** | Global | Climate-related financial disclosure. SDX provides the Scope 1 and 2 metrics and physical risk indicators. |
| **CRREM** | Global | Carbon Risk Real Estate Monitor alignment reporting. |
| **Science Based Targets (SBTi)** | Global | SDX tracks portfolio-level emissions against SBTi targets. |
| **UN SDGs** | Global | Maps building performance to relevant Sustainable Development Goals (7, 11, 12, 13). |

## Compliance status tracker

The **Compliance** tab on your dashboard shows a traffic-light status for each opted-in framework:

- **Green** — All data requirements met and report is ready to generate or has been submitted.
- **Amber** — Some data is missing or requires review. SDX lists the specific gaps.
- **Red** — Significant data gaps or the deadline is within 30 days and the report is not ready.

## Deadline reminders

SDX sends email reminders at 60, 30, and 7 days before each framework's submission deadline. Reminders go to all organisation Owners and Admins. You can customise reminder recipients under **Compliance > Notifications**.

## Direct submission

For frameworks that accept electronic submissions, SDX can submit reports directly:

- NYC LL84 / LL133 via the city's benchmarking portal
- ENERGY STAR Portfolio Manager sync
- GRESB data export in the official template format
