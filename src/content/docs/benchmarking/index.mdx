# Benchmarking Overview

SDX benchmarks every building against up to six international standards using a single data submission. There is no additional configuration, no extra fees, and no duplicate data entry. Submit your utility data once and receive scores across every applicable framework.

## How benchmarking works

1. **You submit data** — Utility readings, floor area, use type, and occupancy details flow into SDX via manual entry, CSV upload, automated feeds, or API. See [Submitting Data](/docs/getting-started/submitting-data).
2. **SDX normalises** — Readings are converted to common units, weather-normalised where required, and aligned to each standard's reporting period.
3. **Peer groups form** — Buildings are grouped by use type, climate zone, and size band to produce fair comparisons.
4. **Scores are calculated** — Each applicable standard's algorithm runs against your normalised data.
5. **Results appear** — Scores, percentile ranks, and letter grades are available on your dashboard within minutes of a data update.

## Supported standards

| Standard | Scope | Score type |
|---|---|---|
| **SDX** | Global, all use types | Percentile rank (1–100) |
| **ENERGY STAR** | US & Canada, eligible use types | Score (1–100) |
| **NABERS** | Australia, office / retail / hotel / data centre | Star rating (1–6) |
| **EU EPC** | European Union, all use types | Letter grade (A–G) |
| **CRREM** | Global, all use types | Stranding year & pathway alignment |
| **GRESB** | Global, all use types | Component score for asset benchmarks |

Not every standard applies to every building. SDX automatically determines which standards are relevant based on the building's location, use type, and available data. See [Benchmarking Standards](/docs/benchmarking/benchmarking-standards) for methodology details.

## Data quality matters

Benchmark accuracy depends on the quality of the underlying data. SDX assigns a [Data Quality Grade](/docs/benchmarking/data-quality-scoring) to every building. Buildings with higher grades receive more reliable scores and are weighted more heavily in aggregate indices.

## Benchmarking is free

All benchmarking features are included in the free SDX platform. There are no premium tiers, no per-building charges, and no feature gates. Every contributor — whether they manage one building or ten thousand — gets the same benchmarking capabilities.

## What you can do with benchmark results

- Compare buildings within your portfolio and against the broader market
- Track year-on-year performance and set reduction targets
- Generate [compliance reports](/docs/benchmarking/reports-and-exports) for regulators and voluntary frameworks
- Share scores with investors, lenders, and tenants via exportable certificates
- Feed scores into your ESG reporting through the [API](/docs/api)
